Privacy Policy

Last updated: 17 April 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Fishing Maps ("the App") collects, uses, and protects your information. By using the App you agree to this policy.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information: When you create an account we collect your email address, used solely to authenticate you via Firebase Authentication.
  • Location data: The App accesses your device's GPS location to display your position on the map. Location data is processed on-device and is not transmitted to our servers.
  • Waypoints and map data: Waypoints and related notes you save are stored in your personal account in Cloud Firestore and are not shared with other users.
  • GPX files: Files you choose to import are read locally on your device. Their content (waypoints) is stored in your account. Raw file data is not retained beyond import.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the App's features.
  • To sync your saved waypoints across devices.
  • To authenticate your account securely.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties except as necessary to operate the App:

  • Firebase (Google LLC): Used for authentication and cloud storage. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

4. Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. You may delete your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us (see below).

5. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including Firebase's built-in encryption in transit and at rest. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

8. Contact
